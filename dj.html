DJ for the Day

Poster design.

Role 

• Visual Design

Breakdown

When I was at Pinnacle Promotions, twice a month we would have a drawing for a DJ For The Day.

The winner would get to play their Spotify™ playlist over the office speakers and they'd get a custom designed 11x17 poster.

I enjoyed getting to know my co-workers styles, personalities and interests better and get to together something they loved.

In the end, we would frame them to hang in our social lounge area creating a pretty great collage of musical memories.
