FiveBox

Recleim/Georgia Power Appliance Pickup Wizard responsive UI design.

Role 

• Wireframes/Production Comps
• UI Design

Breakdown

FiveBox approached me with the honest statement of, "This is what we have. We need this to look like a developer didn't design it. Can you help?"

With almost no criteria, I went straight to Recleim's site, found their style, typefaces and colorschemes to make the web app look and feel the same as the parent company.

With a majority of their consumers using the service via mobile device, I started with the mobile viewport first then worked out.

Final designs* were able to cut the number of pages to 3 down from 5 by critically thinking from a consumers perspective and condensing non pertinent information.

(*Currently under NDA and final production workflow can't be shown.)
